I’m an artist who recently dived into the world of programming. After studying art history, I happened upon a database researcher job for a pair of artists. I fiddled with Excel to organize existing data and plan for future data collection through a user interface. The problem solving and logic involved in my work inspired me to learn to code, which led me to attend Epicodus, a four month intensive bootcamp. Now, I’m looking to utilize my skills in a developer position while expanding my knowledge of different languages, tools and frameworks. With a passion for visual culture and art, I’m eager to take part in creative endeavors through technology.
